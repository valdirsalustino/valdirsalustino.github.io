Hi there,

I'm Valdir, a Data Sciencist with background in High Energy Particle Physics, interested in Big Data, Machine Learning and Statistical Modeling.

In April 2016 I earned a PhD. of Science (High Energy Experimental Particle Physics) from Federal University of Rio de Janeiro, Brazil. My research was fosed on the process of hadronization of the quark production at the LHCb experiment (jet-physics). I have experience in beam instrumentation with contributions to the BGV experiment. If all that sounds interesting, check out my thesis.

After earned the PhD degree, I was granted a postdoctoral fellow position at CBPF (06-2016 to 06-2017) and PUC-Rio (07-2017 to 03-2018) performing Amplitude Analysis in the b-quark and charm-quark sector using LHCb data. After March 2018, I changed to the data science industries. As Data Sciencist, I perform training and evaluation of predictive ML models for default rate of customers in Telecom companies along with other projects of data cleansing and Data Warehouse.
