About Me

Valdir Salustino Guimarães

Pos-doc fellow at Brazilian Center for Research in Physics, Rio de Janeiro - Brazil. My current research field is the CP-Violation phenomena, first discovered in 1964. I have being working in the data analyses of the LHCb experiment for measurements of CP-Violation in charmless tree body decays of the B-hadron. I have bachelor's degree by State University of Rio de Janeiro (2010), Master and PhD degree by Federal University of Rio de Janeiro. I am a member of the LHCb Collaboration.
