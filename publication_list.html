A list of papers I contributed during my academic journey

As posdoc fellow:

  • 2020 - Observation of Several Sources of CP Violation in \(B^{\pm} \rightarrow \pi^{+} \pi^{+} \pi^{-}\) Decays.
  • 2020 - Amplitude Analysis of the \(B^{\pm} \rightarrow \pi^{+} \pi^{+} \pi^{-}\) Decays.
  • 2019 - Amplitude Analysis of \(B^{\pm} \rightarrow \pi^{\pm} K^{+} K^{-}\) Decays.

During my PhD program:

  • 2019 - Noninvasive LHC transverse beam size measurement using inelastic beam-gas interactions.
  • 2017 - Measurement of forward \(t\bar{t}\), \(W+b\bar{b}\) and \(W+c\bar{c}\) production in \(pp\) collisions at \(\sqrt{s}= 8\) TeV.
  • 2017 - First LHC transverse beam size measurements with the beam gas vertex detector.
  • 2017 - First observation of forward \(Z\rightarrow b\bar{b}\) production in \(pp\) collisions at \(\sqrt{s}=8\) TeV.
  • 2017 - Measurement of the bb-quark production cross-section in 7 and 13 TeV \(pp\) collisions.
  • 2016 - Measurement of the forward \(Z\) boson production cross-section in \(pp\) collisions at \(\sqrt{s}=13\) TeV.
  • 2016 - Measurement of forward \(W\) and \(Z\) boson production in association with jets in proton-proton collisions at \(\sqrt{s}= 8\)TeV
  • 2015 - First observation of top quark production in the forward region
  • 2015 - Study of \(W\) boson production in association with beauty and charm
  • 2015 - Identification of beauty and charm quark jets at LHCb.
  • 2015 - Measurement of the Z+b-jet cross-section in \(p\bar{p}\) collisions at \(\sqrt{s} = 7\) TeV in the forward region.

During my M.S program - Physics (2010-2012):

  • 2013 - Bounds on \(Z^{\prime}\) from 3-3-1 model at the LHC energies.

During my B.S program - Physics (2006 - 2010) :

  • 2010 - Estudo do Ruído de Fundo (Background) de Nêutrons e Múons no Detector de Antineutrinos de Angra (portuguese only).
